Victoria Sponge

Bake Time - 20 minutes

Prep Time - 15 minutes

This is my faviourite recipe for a classic victoria sponge. Nothing beats an amazing viccy sponge, i have spent years growing up making these for friends, family, and anyone that wanted one.

"The Best Cake Ever Created!!" - Mollie, 2024

Ingredients

  • 200g Sugar
  • 200g Butter
  • 4 eggs
  • 200g self-raising flour
  • 1 tsp baking powder
  • 2 tbsp milk

Method

STEP 1

heat oven to 190C. butter two 20cm sandwich tins and line with non-stick baking powder

STEP 2

In a large bowl, beat sugar, butter, eggs, flour, baking powder, milk together until you have a smooth, soft batter

STEP 3

Divide the mixture between the tins, smooth the surface with a spatula or the back of a spoon

STEP 4

Bake for 20 minutes until golden and the cake springs back when pressed

STEP 5

Turn onto a cooling rack and leave to cool completely

STEP 6

Add your filling of choice, i recoommend the classic buttercream alond with some jam

STEP 7

ENJOY!!!!!!!!
